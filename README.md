# 🛒 Olist E-commerce Data Analysis (SQL + Python + Power BI)

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

The Brazilian e-commerce platform is facing challenges in delivery performance, customer satisfaction, and seller efficiency.

However, the business lacks clarity on:

* Where delivery delays are occurring
* Which sellers and regions are responsible
* How delivery performance impacts customer experience
* What factors influence revenue and repeat purchases

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze sales performance and revenue trends
* Identify delivery delays and logistics inefficiencies
* Understand customer satisfaction drivers
* Evaluate seller performance and impact on delays
* Analyze payment behavior and customer spending patterns

---

## 🧰 Tools & Technologies

* SQL → Data modeling, cleaning, and feature engineering
* Python (Pandas) → Data analysis and insight generation
* Power BI → Dashboard design and visualization

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

### 🔹 Page 1: Executive Overview

* Total Orders, Revenue, Profit, AOV
* Revenue trend over time
* Category and regional performance

### 🔹 Page 2: Delivery Performance

* On-time vs Late delivery
* Delay by state
* Top delay-causing sellers
* Expected vs Actual delivery trend

### 🔹 Page 3: Customer Satisfaction

* Rating distribution
* Delay vs Rating impact
* Low-rated categories

### 🔹 Page 4: Seller Performance

* Top sellers by revenue
* Sellers causing delays
* Seller rating vs delay

### 🔹 Page 5: Payment Analysis

* Payment type distribution
* Installment behavior
* Average payment value

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Revenue is concentrated in a few product categories, indicating dependency on limited segments
* ~27% of orders are delayed, highlighting operational inefficiencies
* A small group of sellers contributes to the majority of delivery delays
* Delivery delays significantly reduce customer ratings and satisfaction
* Certain states show consistent logistics inefficiencies
* Customer repeat rate is very low (~3%), indicating weak retention
* Top sellers drive revenue but show inconsistent delivery performance
* Credit card is the most used payment method and contributes higher order value
